About Lagos Independent System Operator (LISO)

LISO is committed to ensuring stability, transparency, and efficiency in Lagos' electricity sector. Our leadership drives operational excellence while promoting fair market participation and sustainable growth.

Who We Are

LISO coordinates electricity generation, transmission, and distribution in Lagos State, ensuring a balanced, reliable, and efficient power supply for homes, businesses, and industries. By leveraging real-time data, LISO enables informed decisions and proactive management of the grid.

Licensing & Market Participation

LISO manages licensing frameworks for independent power producers, distribution companies, and other market participants. We ensure that all stakeholders comply with regulations, meet operational standards, and contribute to a fair, transparent, and efficient electricity market.
