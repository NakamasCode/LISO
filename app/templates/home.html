Outages & System Status

Power interruptions may occur due to scheduled maintenance activities, infrastructure upgrades, or unforeseen technical faults within the transmission and distribution network. LISO ensures that all outages are monitored and communicated effectively to minimize uncertainty.

Through real-time updates, stakeholders can access information about affected locations, outage causes, and estimated restoration timelines. This improves transparency and allows businesses and individuals to plan accordingly during service disruptions.

Market, Licenses & Participation

The Lagos electricity market enables collaboration between generation companies, distribution companies, regulators, and independent power producers. LISO plays a central role in coordinating these participants while ensuring fairness, transparency, and operational efficiency.

Participants can access licensing frameworks, compliance requirements, and operational guidelines necessary for entering and operating within the market. This structured environment supports investment, innovation, and long-term sustainability of the power sector.

About LISO & Leadership

The Lagos Independent System Operator is responsible for coordinating electricity generation, transmission, and distribution activities across the state. By maintaining a balance between supply and demand, LISO ensures grid stability and efficient energy delivery.

The organization is guided by experienced leadership and technical experts committed to advancing the power sector. Through strategic planning, operational excellence, and innovation, LISO continues to improve energy access and reliability for millions of residents.
